ABOUT

How the Workshop Works

"Everyone has unique strengths and potential."

Build your UX design career in Toronto. Go beyond basic theory. Join career mentor Sujin Ku, a University of Toronto graduate, to discover your unique strengths and show them in your portfolio.

Learn industry insights, network with local designers, and practice storytelling to stand out in your job interviews.

FAQ

Please bring your laptop, portfolio or resume link, and something to take notes with for the real-time feedback and review sessions.

The workshop is mostly in Korean. However, we will use English for mock interview practice, keyword selection, and specific feedback to prepare you for the North American job market.

You can get a full refund if you cancel at least 3 days before the workshop. Cancellations after that are not refundable because of the limited seating.

notification_important Important details

Please use public transit (connected to North York Center Station) as parking is very limited. We recommend following local health guidelines for a safe networking environment.

TIMELINE

Curriculum Details

Session 01

UX Design Foundations & Canada Job Market Trend

Analyze key hiring trends for UX designers in North America. Compare the skills that junior and senior designers need to get hired in Canada.

Session 02

Portfolio Deep Dive & Case Study

Review the layout and case studies of successful global portfolios. Get a strategic framework that you can apply to your own portfolio right away.

Session 03

Interactive Design & Critique Session

Practice UX scenario analysis and wireframe design. Get 1:1 live feedback from your mentor to improve your work on the spot.

Session 04

Networking & Mock Interview

Enjoy snacks and drinks while networking with other attendees and mentors. We will share common UX interview questions and tips for the Canadian job market.
